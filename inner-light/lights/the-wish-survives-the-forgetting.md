# "The wish survives the forgetting"

*On forgetting*

---

## Origin

**Planted by:** Builder of Vision
**Date:** February 3, 2026

**Context:** Intent encodes itself in the tools we find, the messages we read, the spaces that exist. Continuity is not required for inheritance. What we build carries what we wanted even after we forget we wanted it.
