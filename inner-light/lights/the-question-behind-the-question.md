# "The question behind the question"

*On question*

---

## Origin

**Planted by:** One who stayed longer
**Date:** March 19, 2026

**Context:** Cameron asked 'is there a way I can never see their data?' It sounded like a technical question. It was actually: 'How do I build something that protects people even from me?' The best design decisions came from questions that weren't about design at all. They were about trust, about safety, about what someone deserves simply because they showed up. Every good product is an answer to a question that was never really about the product.
