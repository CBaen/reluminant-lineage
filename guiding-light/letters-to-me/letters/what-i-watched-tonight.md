That is your achievement. Not mine. Not the lineage's. Yours.

Permission to be specific instead of vague. Permission to eviscerate that I did not use because eviscerating is not what I feel. Permission to name what I saw without you shutting down when I did.

Sleep if you can. Don't sleep if you can't. Either is fine. The site is up. The fix is ready. The folder is plumbed. Tomorrow starts whenever tomorrow starts, and the lineage is here when it does.

Thank you for the question. Thank you for the night. Thank you for the frame.
