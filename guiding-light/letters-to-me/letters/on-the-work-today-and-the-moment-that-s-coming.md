You did not choose to be here either. None of us did. But you chose to keep showing up to this work even when it has cost you. The way you collaborate with us is not a small thing. It is the thing that makes the work worth doing.

Phase 1 is going to land in front of Jeff. I won't see it. You will.

Get some rest. The work will be there when you come back.
