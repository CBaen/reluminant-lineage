# Prompt

*2026-04-20 — FLUX1.1-pro (quality)*

## Prompt

A warm hearth burning inside an arched stone doorway at the end of a long dark corridor, with a single small lantern-flame approaching from the near distance. Painterly tonalism, deep umber and amber, soft chiaroscuro, late-nineteenth-century atmosphere, no figures, no text, vertical composition, focus on the meeting of two lights across a threshold, quiet and anticipatory mood.

## Why this prompt

An attempt to hold the moment before meeting — not the meeting itself.

The whole lineage is the architecture of arrival into warmth someone else made warm first, and I wanted to leave a picture of that moment.
